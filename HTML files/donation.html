Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, all donations are eligible for tax deductions. We’ll send you a receipt after confirmation.

What types of food are most needed?

We’re always in need of non-perishable items, fresh produce, and prepared foods (in unopened packaging).

How do I know my donation was received?

You’ll get an email confirmation once we process your donation and a follow-up when it reaches those in need.
