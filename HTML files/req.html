Our Commitment to Confidentiality

Your privacy is important to us. All requests are handled with the utmost confidentiality and respect. We will only share necessary information with trusted food partners to fulfill your request.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a cost for food assistance?

No, all our food assistance is provided free of charge thanks to the generosity of our donors and volunteers.

How quickly will I receive assistance?

We prioritize requests based on urgency and aim to fulfill them within 24-72 hours.

What types of food are available?

We provide a variety of food items, including non-perishable items, fresh produce, and meals, based on availability.
